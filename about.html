Information

- In 2015, over 360,00 King County residents were over 60 years old.
- 78% have one or more chronic medical condition(s)
- 38% have a disability
- 34% are in the work force

- By 2040, over 540,000 King County residents will be over 60.
- The fastest growing segment of the total population are those residents over 85 years old.
